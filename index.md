# Who I am #

I'm a Post-Bac working at the Lawrence Berkeley national lab designing QED modules for the [WarpX Particle in Cell (PIC) simulation software.](https://warpx.readthedocs.io/en/latest/)


 I earned B.As in Physics and Mathematics from the University of California Berkeley in 2019. There, I worked under Prof. Daniel McKinsey on the Xenon Breakdown Apparatus (XeBrA), which was part of the larger Lux-Zeplin (LZ) dark matter collaboration. My undergraduate honors thesis was an initial study of electroluminescence in liquid Argon.

 I'm planning to pursue a PhD in physics and am right now waiting to hear back from several schools. In some respects the wait is harder than the actual applications.

 When not working on physics, I enjoy reading. I'm currently finishing James Islington's **Licanus trilogy**.
